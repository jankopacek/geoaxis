As part of improving our services and increasing the efficiency of our work, we use UAV (drone) technology:

- orthophoto maps
- 3D models for BIM
- digital terrain model creation
- monitoring of deformations and changes in the open countryside
- documentation of forest stands (windfalls, bark beetle damage) and agricultural land (hail, drought, wildlife before harvest) — calculation of loss areas
- inspection of buildings (roofs, chimneys, facades), bridges, dams, rock faces, solar power plants
- filming and photography

Geoaxis holds a permit for aerial work operations and carries statutory insurance for unmanned aircraft including liability insurance.

## We prepare all types of survey plans

- for recording a building or changes to its outer perimeter
- for subdividing a plot or changing a plot boundary
- for recording an easement
- for the course of a staked or owner-confirmed plot boundary

## We stake out the boundaries of your plot

- staking of ownership boundaries
- stabilisation of boundary points (permanent and temporary)
- preparation of staking documentation
- handover of the staked boundary in the field to the owners of the affected plots
